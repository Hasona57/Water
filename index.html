Water Scarcity Awareness

Water scarcity means there needs to be more water for people to use. This is a big problem in many places around the world. The main reasons are not enough rain, too much pollution, and people using too much water. When there is less rain, rivers and lakes dry up, making it hard to get water. Pollution from factories and farms makes water dirty and unsafe. People also use much water for farming and factories, leaving less for others. With enough clean water, it is easier for people to drink, cook, or stay healthy. To help, we need to save water, stop pollution, and use water carefully. We must do something to solve this problem by making a way to condense the water vapor in the air by making a large condensation plate by Peltier, which has a hot side and a cold side. We put a heat sink on the hot side to make the cold side more efficient so it condenses the water vapor from the air, and we add a heat sink on the cold side to increase the surface area.
